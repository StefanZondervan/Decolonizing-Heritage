All I have is an image of you and that's okay
I've never met my grandfather because he abandoned his family while my mom was still young. This is the first photo that I ever saw of him when I was 19, it's him and my grandma on the left. It was a really weird sensation because as soon as I saw the picture I realised I looked like him. I don't know that much about him apart from the fact that he was a con artist, a prick and that we share love for reading. I'm okay with looking like him on the outside, but I wouldn't want to be anything like him otherwise.
